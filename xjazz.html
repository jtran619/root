XJAZZ FESTIVAL

XJAZZ is a festival that strives to cross boundaries. By opening up the jazz spectrum to include improvised electronica and neo classical music. The festival programming places a strong emphasis on contrasting and surprising elements as well as playing in very contrasting venues (techno clubs, indie-rock clubs, punk club, churches).
